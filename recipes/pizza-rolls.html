Pizza Rolls

Just like a pizza

You want a pizza but have no time?
Why don't you make Pizza Rolls just in few minutes?

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Créme fraíche
  • Grated cheese
  • Ham or salami

Steps:

  1. Put the créme fraíche on the pizza dough.
  2. Put the cheese and the ham/salami too.
  3. Roll the dough the way you want.
    (if you want more or thicker pieces)
  4. Bake it in the oven untill dought is baked.

Enjoy!
