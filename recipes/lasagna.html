Lasagna

A delicious italian dish

The easiest way to cook a complicated italian wonder!

Ingredients:

  • 500g Meat
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 TS Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Mix the cooked meat with the crushed tomatoes and
    ad seasonings and spices of your choise.
  3. Start layering the lasagna.
  4. Bake it in the oven till the upper layer of cheese
    lookes baked enough.

Bon Appetit!
