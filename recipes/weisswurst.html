Weißwurst

A delicious bavarian breakfast

A simple recipe with an unbelievable taste!

Ingredients:

  • Weißwurst sausages
  • Tap water
  • mustard (sweet or normal)

Steps:

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Put the Weißwurst sausages into
    the boiling water.
  3. Turn the heat off and let
    the sausages soak for 12 mins.

Enjoy!
